How do I cancel my subscription?

On Android, open the Google Play Store, tap your profile icon, go to Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions, and select Subscription Bill Tracker. On iOS, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and select Subscription Bill Tracker. You can cancel anytime and retain access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Google or Apple account you used to originally subscribe.

Is my data private?

Yes. Subscription Bill Tracker does not require an account and does not connect to your bank. All your bill and subscription data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

What's the difference between free and Premium?

The free version lets you track up to 5 bills or subscriptions. Premium unlocks unlimited tracking, AI savings recommendations, advanced insights, CSV export, and priority support. Premium is available as a monthly ($3.99/mo) or yearly ($24.99/yr) subscription.
